Now

Last updated: 15 Dec 2021

Writing

I’m very close to done with my first poetry manuscript, The Great Permission—a booksite I’m developing using a custom Jekyll theme that Tom Johnson created. I will release it in 2022.

My blog focuses on my technical journey toward completing the book, which will be available online entirely for free, in perpetuity.

Publications

My poem What He Didn’t Know Before was recently published in The West Review

I have two more journal publications forthcoming (hopefully) before the end of 2021:

  • The Mildred Haun Review out of Walter State Community College
  • Poetry Salzburg Review out of the University of Salzburg

Athletics

Still getting my ass kicked on the mats as I relearn how to move my body for jiu-jitsu. At the beginning of 2022, I plan to focus my athletic efforts on martial arts, cutting back on strength training.

Learning Spanish

Current Duolingo streak: 1,271 days (as of 15 Dec 2021)

I’ve been feeling the urge to take group classes again—or find some place where I can speak Spanish with other people. I know I can’t take on another commitment right now, but I’m getting tired of Duolingo. Though my 2021 stats seem respectable:

Other goings on

My godson expressed an interest in soccer, so I bought him some small portable goals & a size 3 soccer ball for Christmas. I can’t wait to play with him for 5 minutes before he gets bored

Really though, I hope soccer can be something we share.
