Now

Last updated: 02 Nov 2021

I turned the heat on today, so open window season is over. This is sad for me in both Summer & Winter because letting the wind blow through my house and hearing my wind chime is a small, calming pleasure in my life.

Writing

I’m very close to done with my first poetry manuscript, The Great Permission—a booksite I’m developing using a custom Jekyll theme that Tom Johnson created.

My blog focuses on my technical journey toward completing the book, which will be available online entirely for free, in perpetuity.

Forthcoming

I have two journal publications forthcoming before the end of the year:

  • The Mildred Haun Review out of Walter State Community College
  • Poetry Salzburg Review out of the University of Salzburg

Athletics

I practice Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, focusing on the no-gi variety. Four months in, I’m a white belt, but don’t know if I can advance since I don’t train at all in a gi. Also, you know, I don’t actually wear a belt. I’m told it typically takes 12–18 months to earn a blue belt.

I recently ended a powerlifting cycle. I say “ended” rather than “completed” because I had to cut it short at 10 weeks (instead of my planned 12) when I strained a ligament in my wrist. It was my first cycle since the end of 2019 (because: pandemic), and unfortunately, I only set one Personal Record (PR): a new barbell hip thrust 1RM of 465lb (211kg).

Now I’m focusing on metabolic conditioning to augment my BJJ training.

Learning Spanish

Current Duolingo streak: 1,228 days (as of 02 Nov 2021)

I’ve been studying Spanish for 3.5 years. I took group classes at the Tennessee Language Center for two years, but after they switched to online classes during the pandemic, I felt like I wasn’t engaging with the format, and didn’t register for the next session. Also, I have a corporate job, and participate in my fill of video calls during the work week.

My Duolingo streak is entirely Spanish study. It’s obviously not sufficient for becoming fluent, but it’s the engagement I have the capacity for right now. If I get the opportunity for a sabbatical or extended travel in the future, my plan is to live in a Spanish-speaking country.

Other goings on

I recently attended my first wedding since the pandemic, and it was gorgeous. My buddy, author Joe Seale, got married on the outskirts of the Smoky Mountains, in October. If you’re not from an SEC town, the Saturdays during away football games are coveted wedding dates.

I also just replayed Final Fantasy VII (for the first time in probably 15 years) on PS4. The x3 fast-forward feature was great! I bred my gold chocobo in one afternoon!
