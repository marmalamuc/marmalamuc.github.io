Athletics

Still trying to figure out out to balance lifting & BJJ. I’m 36, and both are tough on my body now. I love powerlifting as a solitary, mindful pursuit, and appreciate BJJ for its practical application (although I would do everything in my power to avoid a fight).

I need more recovery time these days, and trying to progress in both is difficult. It seems soft tissue injuries rotate around my body; right now, it’s tendonitis in one elbow, some kind of impingement behind one kneecap (where I’ve previously torn my LCL), & a new pain on the anterior side of the other knee.

Learning Spanish

Current Duolingo streak: 1,310 days (as of 23 Jan 2022 )

Been reviewing the past perfect lately (have + past participle). I’ve used it before in the group classes I used to take, but I really like Duolingo’s method of introducing it without asking you to think about the prescriptive theory of it. It’s less academic, which is great because I just want to speak, be understood, & understand; like English, I don’t care what a style guide or the Royal Spanish Academy prescribes.

Other goings on

Technology

Getting a new computer has been a pain in the ass. I needed a clean migration to avoid transferring whatever was causing kernel panics on the old computer, but every step has been a headache.

I set up an admin user & standard user as a security measure, but I don’t think it’s been worth fucking with all the path variables and switching back & forth between users to manage installations & updates. Open source is such a headache.

Also, Mac deprecated PHP support, so my setup for submissions is obsolete now. Git submodules might be the solution, but that’s one more thing to research & test before I can just get back to what I was doing on my old computer.

Buffalo Bills

The Bills play the Chiefs tonight in the AFC Divisional playoffs. It’s risky to publish this the day of, but I’m calling the Bills win today, next week hosting the Bengals, and two weeks after that in the Super Bowl against the Rams.

I don’t believe in curses or superstition, and don’t feel apprehensive at all about saying that.
