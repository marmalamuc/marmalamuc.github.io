Now

Last updated: 03 Jul 2022

Writing

The Great Permission really is still in progress. I swear. I finally finished a draft of the most crucial poem in the booksite, which means I can start sending everything out for review soon.

TGP will be online in 2022.

Athletics

Most of my workouts over the past two months have been metabolic conditioning (“metcons” if you’re a crossfitter), including time on the track for sprints.

I haven’t been consistent with jiu-jitsu recently (again). I love being on the mat, but something about the fixed schedule of times makes it less likely that I’ll attend. Or, it’s that I’m drawn the solo nature of strength training & running. I do want to be alone most of the time.

Learning Spanish

Current Duolingo streak: 1,471 days (as of 03 Jul 2022 )

I just passed the official four year mark at 1,461 days (which includes one leap year). For the first two years, I also took group language classes through my company’s educational reimbursement program, but left when the classes had to switch to online-only for the pandemic.

I also started studying a new language—Dutch. More on that below.

Other goings on

New opportunities

After a big reorganization within the business unit at my company, they eliminated several positions, and mine was one. Not a big surprise, since I was a Technical Writer at a company without that position in their career architecture. I had a couple opportunities to transition to other internal positions, but they would have required me to refocus as more of a Business Analyst or Project Manager, so I decided to take a severance and start a wider job search.

I’m mostly looking in Europe since American democracy has been sabotaged by an authoritarian theocracy. Since the end of May, I’ve had… so many interviews. Two positions are located in Amsterdam, hence my recent interest in the Dutch language.

Learning

I’ve been learning JavaScript by working through some sample projects. They’re all in the sandbox now, but I’ll probably roll out some simple features on this website when I’m a bit more polished.

Related to my job search, I’ve also been taking Tom Johnson’s Documenting APIs course. Doc for developers is one of the biggest trends in techcom, and I’m trying to stay with the curve. I tend to thrive in the minutia, so I’m feeling right at home with endpoints & cURL.
