Now

Last updated: 15 Jul 2022

Writing

The Great Permission really is still in progress. I swear. I finally finished a draft of the most crucial poem in the booksite, which means I can start sending everything out for review soon. TGP will be online in 2022.

Otherwise, I’ve been writing semi-consistently this year, and have several new drafts. Almost every poem deals (directly or tangentially) with America’s accelerating transformation into a necropolitical state. As individual poems, I’m happy with them, but they don’t fit into the booksite. If I ever create a second manuscript, they might end up there.

Other goings on

Job hunting

Job interviews are crazy these days. For all the talk about labor shortages & unfilled positions, employers sure take their time progressing the hiring timeline.

I recently had an interview in which the recruiter asked to see writing samples. I don’t have any from my most recent employer because of various governmental & corporate restrictions, but since my blog elucidates my tech journey, it’s close to the kind of work I would do as a tech writer. They asked me to send the link, and I did. So we’ll see how that works out, given my language & political stances & general attitude toward the concept of work
