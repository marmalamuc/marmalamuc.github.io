Now

Last updated: 23 Apr 2022

Writing

My booksite, The Great Permission, is still in progress, and will be released in 2022.

I’ve been making progress on some modest publicity efforts. I’m still in the planning phase, but have been in contact with a couple professionals in the book publicity space, and now have a rough timeline that would have the booksite online in late summer or early fall.

Publications

Publications this calendar year:

  • Two poems in Poetry Salzburg Review
  • Five poems in The Mildred Haun Review
  • In memoriam in The Wild Word
  • Dead Letters (to Brandon) in Stirring

Nothing forthcoming right now! I’ve been focused on the booksite & related efforts, and haven’t been submitting for regular journal reading periods.

Athletics

Not much happening here. Haven’t been consistent with BJJ the past month, and my solo workouts have been sporadic. I’m taking a lot more walks, but not exercising much, per se.

Learning Spanish

Current Duolingo streak: 1,400 days (as of 23 Apr 2022 )

Finally learning the future tense. So far just the simple future: infinitive + é/ás/á/emos/án.

Not sure how complicated future tenses can get. If it’s as nuanced as the past tenses, I’ve got months before I’ll have a grasp on it.

Other goings on

Travel

Albuquerque was beautiful. And all the public transportation was free! A lot of restaurants had “New Mexican food” on their menu, but I couldn’t tell what distinguishes it from TexMex (other than maybe the prominence of green chile). Also, there were a ton of Middle Eastern & Mediterranean restaurants—like, five in the 1mi2 area around my Airbnb.

Writing vacations usually reignite my motivation, even after I return home; I’m hoping I can extend my focus to power through some of the many tasks left on my booksite To Do.
