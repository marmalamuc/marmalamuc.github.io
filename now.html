Now

Last updated: 17 May 2022

Writing

The Great Permission is still in progress. I’ve been a bit sidetracked lately due to some other efforts, but all related to the booksite.

In any case, TGP will be online in 2022—if for no other reason than I’m tired of thinking about it.

Athletics

I’m back to my regular weekly BJJ training. I got a bad bruise in one ear recently, and had to start wearing headgear to prevent cauliflower ear. It’s uncomfortable, but I’m sure I’ll get used to it.

Learning Spanish

Current Duolingo streak: 1,424 days (as of 17 May 2022 )

Still practicing only with Duolingo. I wish I had more time to devote to language learning—specifically speaking & listening—but I’m doing what I’m capable of now. And when I look back at where I was 3, 6, 9, 12 months ago, I see clear progress.
