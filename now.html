Now

Last updated: 28 Jan 2023

Writing

Surprisingly, I’ve written several drafts since landing. Surprising because I’ve had so much to take care of as a new arrival. But the new place & experiences have been very generative. One day soon enough, I’ll be ready to submit again; some of these new drafts are the best poems I’ve written in years, and I think they’re almost ready for homes.

I’ve already found two artist events where I’m becoming a regular:

  • Labyrinth Open Mic Monday
  • BODY ELECTRIC

Still vainly hoping for book reviews & interviews of The Great Permission. Contact me if you’d like to review or talk about TGP.

Languages

Current Duolingo streak: 1,680 days (as of 28 Jan 2023 )

Daily Dutch lessons on Duolingo. My new employer had offered Dutch classes in the office, but canceled that benefit shortly before I started. So, I’ll be looking for a language tutor on iTalki, which was recommended by a friend who uses it to learn Korean (yes, he is fluent).

Other goings on

Expatriation

I live in Amsterdam! For three months now. Almost all my energy has gone toward setting up the basic things I need to live, like:

  • apartment
  • bank
  • healthcare
  • phone

Plus getting comfortable at my new job. I’m still completely charmed by the city; I love wandering around on foot or bike. But I haven’t done much exploring. The daylight hours are quite short in the winter, so I’m mostly waiting for spring.

In the meantime, I eat a lot of pastries and drink a lot of coffee.
