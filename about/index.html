About

Andrew P. Dillon earned his BA from the University of Tennessee, and later graduated in their inaugural MFA class. He was awarded a scholarship to the Virginia Center for Creative Arts workshop in Auvillar, France, and has been nominated for the Best New Poets anthology & a Pushcart Prize.

His work is forthcoming or has appeared most recently in Poetry Salzburg Review, Please See Me, Second Chance Lit, Analog, Stirring, & Connotation Press. He lives in Nashville, and is completing his first collection, The Great Permission.
