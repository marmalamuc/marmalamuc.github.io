Agenda

Open the curtains
charge your phone
wash your face
dress in the outfit
you picked last night
feed the cats
blend your smoothie
pull out the chicken
to defrost
lock the door

4 deep breaths
hold 7s
exhale 8
don't crash the bike
don't slip into
the ribcage of canals

open the laptop
push words
from one file
to another
meditate in the quiet
rooms we now
acknowledge
increase
productivity
sell your labor
for capital &
accrual of
holidays

4 deep breaths
hold 7s
exhale 8
don't crash the bike
don't ride into
the ribcage of canals

Chop the veggies
wash the dishes
dry, replace them
in drawers
no one argues over
listen intentionally
to an album from
your childhood

4 deep breaths
hold 7s
exhale 8
don't crash the bike
don't inhale
the current riding
the ribcage of canals

Buy hash, wine, shrooms
any poison
step out
into the basin
of a country
where no wind carries
your name
no body
craves your touch
your scent
watering
their mouth

4 deep breaths
hold 7s
exhale 8
don't crash
don't slip
over the rail
don't watch
the ribcage of canals
carry the name
from your lips
